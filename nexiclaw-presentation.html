The Semantic Gap in Phase 0

The "Flattening" Bottleneck

The Goal
Moving Pluralistic Values into AtomSpace

Moving diverse, pluralistic human values from real-world interviews into the OpenCog Hyperon AtomSpace for reasoning over with NAL / PLN.

The Flaw
LLMs "Flatten" Localized Knowledge

Standard LLMs compress culturally-specific values into generic Western jargon — e.g., turning "Generational Land Security" into "Data Safety."

The Incompatibility
Unstructured Transcripts ≠ Reasoning-Ready

Raw interview transcripts cannot be reasoned over by symbolic logic frameworks like NAL or PLN without translation to S-expressions.

Current (Broken) Pipeline
🎙 Human Interview Rich cultural context, lived experience
↓
🤖 Standard LLM No cultural audit layer
↓
⚠ Generic Output "Data Safety" — nuance destroyed
↓
✗ AtomSpace Incompatible Cannot reason over plain text
Architecture Overview

The Reflexive Compiler Architecture

A two-stage pipeline designed to preserve human friction and generate machine-legible logic.

01
Stage 1: Grounded Extraction
Forces the model to act as a localized qualitative researcher, explicitly auditing language against corporate-speak to map systemic power asymmetries and extract genuine cultural values.
↓
02
Stage 2: Symbolic Compilation
Translates audited insights into strictly validated MeTTa S-expressions — ContextNode, ValueNode, ConceptNode, FrictionLink — ready for direct AtomSpace injection.
